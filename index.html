PROTOCOL QUESTIONS

What is live at MVP launch?

The initial launch includes FLUX minting, FluxFi dashboard, live runtime agents, and Base market tooling with autonomous workflows.

Protocol fees are partially burned and partially allocated to runtime infrastructure, ecosystem growth, and liquidity operations.

Yes. Governance, staking, cross-chain settlement, and public agent marketplace modules are planned in future phases.
